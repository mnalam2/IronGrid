02 Process

From first call to launch day.

You don't need to speak tech — that's our job. A clear four-step process takes you from idea to a live website, with no surprises.

  1. 01

    Discover

    A short call about your business, your customers, and what you need the site to do. You get a flat written quote with a timeline — not a vague estimate, and never an hourly meter.

  2. 02

    Design

    You see real designs of your actual site — homepage first — and we revise until you love it. Nothing gets built until you've approved how it looks.

  3. 03

    Build

    We build it fast, mobile-friendly, and search-ready, and you watch progress on a live preview link the whole way. Questions answered in plain English, same day.

  4. 04

    Launch

    We handle the domain, hosting, email, and analytics, then hand you the keys with a walkthrough. Want us to keep maintaining it? Care plans are available — but never required.
