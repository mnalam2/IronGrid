FAQ

        
Straight answers.

        

          
How fast can you start?+
Discovery calls happen within days of your first email; most engagements begin within one to three weeks. If your date is hard, say so — we'll tell you honestly whether we can hit it.

          
How do engagements work?+
Fixed-price projects against a written scope, monthly staff augmentation per engineer, or a retained partnership with reserved capacity. All under a short MSA with 30-day exit terms on ongoing work.

          
What do you charge?+
Every project gets a flat written quote before anything starts — no hourly meters, no hidden fees. Tell us what you need and you'll have a real number within a business day.

          
Can you work with our existing team and codebase?+
Yes — embedding in existing teams and rescuing existing systems is half our work. Your repos, your tracker, your standup.

          
Do you have client references?+
We're a new studio, so we show working proof instead: this site and the commitments we sign. You'll see real output before you commit a dollar — the only thing you're trusting up front is a free call.
